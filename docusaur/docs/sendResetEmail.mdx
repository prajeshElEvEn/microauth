<p>Sends a password reset email to the provided email address.</p>

**Returns**: Promise&lt;{message: string}&gt;
- <p>A Promise that resolves with a success message if the email is sent.</p>

|PARAMETER|TYPE|OPTIONAL|DEFAULT|DESCRIPTION|
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
|email|string|||<p>The email address to which the reset email will be sent.</p>|
|resetToken|string|||<p>The reset token to include in the email.</p>|
